Biography

I am a second-year PhD student supervised by Prof. Otmar Hilliges and Prof. Dimitris Tzionas. I obtained my Master's degree from ETH Zürich and my Bachelor's degree from Beihang University.

I am interested in digital humans and neural fields, with a goal to build learning-based methods that can faithfully model human-scene interaction.
