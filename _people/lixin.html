Biography

I am a PhD student supervised by Prof. Otmar Hilliges, Prof. Dimitris Tzionas, and Prof. Marc Pollefeys. I obtained my Master's degree from ETH Zürich and my Bachelor's degree from Beihang University.

My research interests mainly lie in computer vision and computer graphics, especially in the reconstruction of human-object interactions.
