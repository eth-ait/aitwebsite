About Me

I graduated with a Ph.D. degree from Advanced Interactive Technologies lab at ETH Zurich with funding from the Max Planck ETH Center for Learning Systems (CLS). I was supervised by Michael J. Black (MPI) and Otmar Hilliges (ETH). Now, I work as an ML Scientist at Meshcapade.
